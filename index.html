Smiley: A Social Wellbeing App
Introducing Smiley, the social media app that helps build a non-toxic community and promotes mental well-being. Our app is designed to create a positive and uplifting space where users can share one thing that kept them positive for the day and send a selfie of themselves smiling. By eliminating the 'like' and 'comment' functions, we're promoting less toxicity and less addiction. But that's not all, Smiley also features a live-time map that shows how many users are using the app and smiling around you, which fosters a sense of connectedness and community. Our research shows that mental illness is on the rise globally, and social media can exacerbate negative feelings and emotions. But with Smiley, we're creating a solution that promotes social bonding, positivity, and well-being. Join us in our mission to build a healthier, happier, and more connected world with Smiley!
